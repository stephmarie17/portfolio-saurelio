About Me

I'm a full-stack developer based in Los Angeles, CA. The first website I ever made was a Sailor Moon fansite in the mid-90's. I took a brief detour from the web to become a librarian, served as a public librarian for four years, and now freelance as a full-stack developer.

If I'm not sitting at my laptop optimizing my VSCode themes, you can find me at one of the many dog-friendly restaurants in LA with my Shih Tzu, reading a good book with a strong cup of coffee, or baking something completely unhealthy.
